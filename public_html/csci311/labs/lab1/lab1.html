How To Spot A Canadian

Expectations vs Reality

Stereotypes of Canadians are hilarious and sometimes painfully true, but sorry they're not all accurate. Unfortunetly, we don't live in igloos and ice skate to work to then pour a big glass of maple syrup. When we're out and about we do go to Timmies and buy a double double with some loonies and toonies though.
